Why We Exist

The issues regarding mental health have lately drawn a lot of attention. A large number of people need support and guidance regarding mental health due to many misconceptions that exist regarding this topic. This web application provides users with an easy access resource hub which consists of multiple blogs and articles regarding mental health, different issues, misconception, and so on. One of the major features is a chatbot which can respond to user queries and provide general guidance as well as information regarding mental health. Furthermore, this website aims to address the growing need for accessible mental health assistance by providing a medium for people to connect with health experts and professionals.

The main purpose of this website is to address the expanding need for mental health awareness and resources. A lot of people face mental health issues and challenges but they are unable to ask help from others.
